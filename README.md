## 📌 Project Overview

Mental health disorders such as chronic depression are influenced by multiple demographic, behavioral, and medical factors. This project performs a complete exploratory data analysis on a large healthcare dataset to uncover meaningful patterns and provide actionable insights through SQL queries, Python-based EDA, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The project demonstrates a complete data analytics workflow starting from data cleaning and exploration to business intelligence visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze demographic and lifestyle characteristics associated with depression.
- Discover patterns among medical history and behavioral habits.
- Perform data cleaning and exploratory data analysis using Python.
- Extract business insights using SQL.
- Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for data-driven decision making.

# 🔍 Key Insights

- Income distribution is highly right-skewed, with the majority of individuals earning below the upper-income range.
- Smoking and alcohol consumption frequently co-occur with lower levels of physical activity.
- Individuals reporting chronic medical conditions often also report a family history of depression.
- Lifestyle behaviors such as smoking, poor dietary habits, and inadequate physical activity contribute significantly to elevated risk segments.
- Income exhibits only a weak correlation with mental illness history, indicating that mental health outcomes are influenced by multiple interacting demographic and behavioral factors.
- Sleep patterns and physical activity show meaningful associations, highlighting the importance of healthy lifestyle choices in mental well-being.
